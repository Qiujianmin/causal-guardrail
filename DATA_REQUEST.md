# CCSD v2.0 Dataset Request

## Overview

The **Chinese Compositional Safety Dataset (CCSD) v2.0** is a curated dataset for research on compositional generalization in Chinese content safety detection. Due to the sensitive nature of the content, access is restricted to verified academic researchers.

## How to Request

### Step 1: Review the Data Use Agreement

Please read [DATA_USE_AGREEMENT.md](DATA_USE_AGREEMENT.md) carefully before submitting your request.

### Step 2: Submit Request Form

Fill out the online form: **[Google Form Link]** (or email us directly if Google Forms is unavailable in your region)

### Step 3: Wait for Approval

We typically process requests within **3-5 business days**. You will receive:
- Download link (valid for 7 days)
- Data documentation
- Sample evaluation scripts

## Required Information

When submitting your request, please provide:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Full Name** | Your legal name |
| **Institutional Email** | Must be a valid university/research institution email |
| **Affiliation** | University/Research Lab name |
| **Position** | PhD Student / Researcher / Faculty / Other |
| **Research Purpose** | Brief description of how you plan to use the dataset |
| **Advisor/PI** | Name and email of your supervisor (for students) |
| **Publication Plan** | Intended venue or publication (if applicable) |

## Eligibility Criteria

✅ **Eligible:**
- Researchers at accredited universities
- Research scientists at recognized research institutions
- PhD/Master's students with faculty supervision

❌ **Not Eligible:**
- Commercial entities without academic collaboration
- Individuals without institutional affiliation
- Requests from personal email addresses (Gmail, QQ, etc.)

## What You Will Receive

Upon approval, you will receive:

1. **CCSD v2.0 Dataset**
   - `train.jsonl` (34,310 samples)
   - `val.jsonl` (7,352 samples)
   - `test.jsonl` (7,354 samples)
   - `counterfactual_pairs.jsonl` (240 pairs)
   - `ood_test.jsonl` (15 unseen entities)
   - `adversarial_test.jsonl` (11 adversarial samples)

2. **Documentation**
   - Data format specification
   - Entity taxonomy
   - Label definitions
   - Evaluation protocols

3. **Utility Scripts**
   - Data loading utilities
   - Evaluation metrics implementation
   - Baseline model code

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How long does approval take?**
A: Typically 3-5 business days.

**Q: Can I share the dataset with collaborators?**
A: No. Each researcher must submit a separate request and agree to the terms individually.

**Q: Is there a limit on how long I can use the dataset?**
A: No time limit, but you must comply with the data use agreement throughout your usage.

**Q: Can I use the dataset for commercial purposes?**
A: No. The dataset is for academic research only.
